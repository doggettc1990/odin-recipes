Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Description:

Try this delicious Slow Cooker Pulled Pork that tastes almost identical to Texas BBQ without all the effort of running a smoker!

Just throw it in the slow cooker and forget about it until later! Once it's done, shred it up and toss it on a bun for a delicious pulled pork sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • 1 TSP Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Pork Shoulder Roast (About 4 Pounds)
  • 1 Cup of BBQ Sauce (Your Favorite!)
  • 1/2 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1/2 Cup Chicken Broth
  • 1/4 Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • 1 TBSP Yellow Mustard
  • 1 TBSP Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 TBSP Chili Powder
  • 1 Medium Onion (Chopped)
  • 2 Cloves Garlic (Minced)
  • 8 Hamburger Buns
  • Butter (As Needed)

Steps:

  1. Pour vegetable oil into slow cooker, followed by pork roast. Add BBQ sauce, vinegar, and chicken broth. Stir in brown sugar, yellow mustard, Worcestershire, chili powder, onion, and garlic.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 10 to 12 hours, or high for 5 to 6 hours, until pork shreds easily with a fork.
  3. Remove pork from the slow cooker, and shred the meat. Return meat to slow cooker, and mix in with remaining juices.
  4. Butter and toast buns, and serve with pork on top.
  5. ENJOY!!!
