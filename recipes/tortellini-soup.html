Sausage and Tortellini Soup

Description:

Check out this easy Sausage and Tortellini Soup!

Ready in less than 20 minutes with less than

Ingredients:

  • 1 Package Cheese Tortellini (Not Frozen)
  • 1 Medium Onion, Diced
  • 4 oz. Fresh Mushrooms, Sliced
  • 1 Large Can Italian Style Diced Tomatoes
  • 2 Small Cans Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Pack Italian Sausage Links (I Use Hot)
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder, and Italian Seasoning to Taste
  • Olive Oil
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Put sausage in a pan with olive oil on medium heat.
  2. Add onion, mushrooms, and seasonings to pan and cover.
  3. Once sausage is cooked about halfway remove from pan.
  4. Add tomatoes and tomato sauce to pan. Add water equivalient to to the can of diced tomatoes.
  5. Slice sausage into thin medallions and add back to soup.
  6. Return to light boil and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Reduce heat and add tortellini to soup.
  8. Cook about 5 minutes and serve! Soup will be hot!!!
