Spaghetti and Marinara with Italian Sausage

Description:

This spaghetti and marinara is super easy and take less than 30 minutes to prepare!

Using italian sausage instead of ground beef adds a flavor to this marinara that is unrivaled!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Box Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1 Large Jar Traditional Marinara from Grocery Store
  • 1 Pack Hot Italian Sausage Links
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 1/4 Cup Balsalmic Vinegar
  • Italian Seasonings, Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, and Chili Powder to Taste
  • 1 TBSP Honey
  • 1 TBSP Texas Pete Hot Sauce
  • One Stick of Butter

Steps:

  1. Melt butter in a large pan over low heat.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and sausage to pan.
  3. Raise to medium heat and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove sausuage and add marinara, seasonings, vinegar, honey, and hot sauce to pan. Stir well.
  5. In a pot start boiling water for your pasta. When water boils add pasta and cook until al dente.
  6. While water is boiling and sauce is cooking, slice sausage into thin medallions and add back to sauce.
  7. Cook about 10 minutes until sausage and totally cooked.
  8. Serve sauce over pasta and enjoy!
